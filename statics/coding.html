coding and problem solving

Coding is the art of translating ideas into instructions for computers. Problem-solving in coding involves breaking down complex problems into manageable steps, devising efficient algorithms, and debugging. It's a creative process that requires logical thinking, perseverance, and continuous learning, fostering a mindset to tackle challenges and build innovative solutions.
